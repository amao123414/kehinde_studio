  • The Client must be 18 years of age or older to enter into a commission agreement. If the Client is under 18, a parent or legal guardian must submit and sign the contract on their behalf.
  • The Artist reserves the right to refuse any commission for any reason without explanation.
  • The Artist is not responsible for any adverse reactions (e.g., allergies) related to the materials used in the finished product.

2. Quoting and Pricing

All prices listed on the website are *estimates only*. The final price will be determined by the complexity of the design, material costs, and any requested features (LEDs, complex patterns, etc.).

  • Quotes are valid for 60 days. After 60 days, a new quote may be required to reflect current pricing and material costs.
  • Any significant changes requested by the Client after the deposit is paid may incur additional fees.

3. Payment and Deposits

A *non-refundable deposit* is required to secure your commission slot and is applied toward the final cost of the project. This deposit covers initial material sourcing and design time.

  • *Deposit:* The required deposit amount will be specified in the quote and is non-refundable upon cancellation by the Client.
  • *Payment Plans:* Flexible payment plans are available. Full payment must be received before work begins on the physical creation phase.
  • *Failure to Pay:* If the Client fails to make scheduled payments without prior arrangement, the commission may be canceled, and all previous payments (excluding the non-refundable deposit) will be forfeit.

4. Commission Process

Commissions are completed in the order they were agreed upon and paid for. Estimated completion dates are subject to change due to unexpected delays (e.g., material shipping, conventions, illness).

  • WIP (Work in Progress) Updates: The Client will receive updates and approval checks at key stages (e.g., modeling, base completion, furring start).
  • Changes: Only minor changes that do not affect the quoted price or original concept are permitted after the physical creation has begun. Major changes will incur a fee and may extend the deadline.
  • Cancellation by Artist: In the rare event the Artist must cancel the commission, a full refund, including the deposit, will be issued to the Client.

The Artist offers a 30-day warranty on all structural seams and components, beginning on the date of delivery. This warranty covers damage due to faulty craftsmanship.

  • The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, improper cleaning, rough housing, or accidental damage.
  • After the 30-day warranty period, the Client is responsible for all repair costs, including shipping fees.
